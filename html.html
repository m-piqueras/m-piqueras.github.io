Sobre mí

Soy un ingeniero de telecomunicaciones apasionado por el aprendizaje continuo y la innovación tecnológica. Mi experiencia se centra en áreas como sonido e imagen, electrónica, sistemas y telemática , aplicando estos conocimientos para resolver problemas complejos y desarrollar soluciones prácticas y eficientes.

A lo largo de mi trayectoria académica, he participado en una amplia variedad de proyectos, incluyendo el diseño de antenas, programación de FPGAs, placas electrónicas, acústica, codificación y decodificación de señales, análisis de descriptores aplicados a imágenes, redes neuronales, diarización de locutores, encriptación y desencriptación de datos y análisis de tráfico de datos. Estas iniciativas han involucrado el uso de herramientas y lenguajes como Python, MatLab, Xilinx, lenguaje ensamblador, C, Google Colab, Wireshark, ADS, CST, terminal de Linux y máquinas virtuales , consolidando mi capacidad para abordar desafíos multidisciplinarios y coordinar y liderar grupos de personas de manera efectiva.

Key Projects

Academic and personal projects demonstrating technical proficiency.

Bachelor's Thesis (TFG)

  • Designed a neural network architecture to predict turn-taking changes in audio recordings based on acoustic feature analysis.
  • Engineered a rigorous data preprocessing pipeline to tabularize and format acoustic inputs for optimal model training.
  • Developed a custom post-processing module to refine predictions and visualize results with high precision.

FPGA Audio System (HW/SW Co-Design)

  • Developed a real-time audio recording and playback system using Vivado and Simulink for hardware synthesis.
  • Implemented embedded control software in C using Vitis to manage audio codecs and data flow.
  • Integrated on-board peripherals to enable physical volume control and recording/playback mode switching directly from the FPGA board.

Network Planning & Traffic Analysis

  • Deployed network collectors and exporters to gather real-time telemetry and visualize traffic flow data.
  • Conducted deep packet inspection and flow analysis to identify bottlenecks, protocols, and usage patterns.
  • Dimensioned the network infrastructure and generated a cost-optimized, real-world budget proposal based on capacity requirements.

Machine Learning & Signal Processing

  • Implemented K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) algorithms combined with Dynamic Time Warping (DTW) to classify temporal audio sequences.
  • Designed and trained Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) for music genre classification and speech/music discrimination.
  • Performed feature extraction using Mel-frequency cepstral coefficients (MFCCs) and Chroma features to optimize classifier accuracy.

Digital Video Broadcasting

  • Analyzed MPEG-2 Transport Streams (TS) structure, verifying compliance with DVB broadcasting standards.
  • Decoded and interpreted PSI/SI tables (PAT, PMT, SDT) to identify multiplexing structures and service configurations.
  • Evaluated Quality of Service (QoS) metrics including PCR jitter and packet loss to diagnose transmission integrity issues.

Computer Vision & Image Processing

  • Developed an image search engine capable of retrieving similar images from a dataset based on visual content rather than metadata.
  • Implemented global and local descriptors (Color Histograms, Texture features) to generate unique image signatures.
  • Optimized search efficiency using Euclidean distance metrics and validated system performance through Precision and Recall analysis.

RF & Microwave Engineering

  • Designed and simulated a microstrip patch antenna array using CST Studio Suite tailored for specific frequency bands.
  • Optimized key RF parameters including Return Loss ($S_{11}$), Voltage Standing Wave Ratio (VSWR), and Radiation Patterns.
  • Analyzed electromagnetic field distributions and current density to maximize gain and directivity for wireless communications.
